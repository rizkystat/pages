I. Litigation and Dispute Settlement

1. Criminal Law
We offer legal assistance in criminal litigation supported by extensive experience in assisting and representing clients at all stages of criminal proceedings — from police investigation to trial at the District Court. We handle a wide range of criminal matters involving individuals and corporations, including cases related to banking crimes, finance, insurance, investment, shareholder disputes, commercial and construction contracts, energy and natural resources, plantations, narcotics, fraud, and other criminal offenses.

2. Commercial Law
We have substantial experience representing clients in various commercial litigation disputes in Indonesia, including matters related to banking, finance, contracts, development, and unlawful acts. We have assisted numerous clients in the Indonesian Courts, including major banking institutions in disputes between banks and customers. Our expertise includes defending clients in contractual agreement disputes.

3. Intellectual Property Rights
We are experienced in handling intellectual property litigation as part of our commitment to protecting clients’ intellectual property assets. We have been involved in several court cases related to patents, trademarks, industrial designs, and copyrights.

4. Industrial Relations Disputes
We assist clients in employment-related issues, particularly regarding termination of employment contracts, including negotiation outside the court. We address matters involving the rights and obligations between companies and employees. While we prioritize amicable settlement, we are also experienced in resolving employment disputes faced by workers and corporations.

5. State Administration
We have extensive experience representing clients at the State Administrative Court in disputes involving the Central Government, Local Government, companies, and individuals. In handling these cases, we always prioritize peaceful alternative solutions to support and protect the client's business interests.

II.Intellectual property rights

Intellectual property rights We have experience in providing consultation and managing Intellectual Property business asset protection in Indonesia. We have an experienced team in practice in trademark, industrial design, copyright and patent. We serve and assist clients for consulting, searching, signing up, brand switching, industrial design, patents and copyrights.

III.Bankruptcy

We have experience of processing bankruptcy and bankruptcy procedures, we act for clients in the settlement of bankruptcy disputes and debt payment delays and we review the settlement in all aspects of the corporate bankruptcy debate, including but not limited to enforcing the law and assessing the proposed restructuring or survive corporate restructuring and re-organization. Our team are also certified as the recipient or has been appointed by Commercial Court as Management or Curator in the settlement of Bankruptcy case in Indonesia.

Providing legal services in the field of commercial corporations that we have provided legal assistance in the field of commercial corporations faced by clients throughout Indonesia, private as well as companies, multinational corporations, BUMN , as well as government agencies and institutions in relation to corporations and commercial transactions. We have a team of lawyers who have expertise and experience in various types ranging from local companies, investment, banking, finance, capital markets, energy and natural resources, infrastructure, real estate and development as well as all types of commercial transactions including but not limited we also provide legal opinion to corporation related to corporate action.
